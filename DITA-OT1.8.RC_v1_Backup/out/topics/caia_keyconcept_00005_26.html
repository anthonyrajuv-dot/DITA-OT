LO 17.3

REIT returns reveal a wider return and risk distribution than the NPI and FTSE NAREIT U.S. Real Estate Index Series indices over a 25-year period. This may be a function of smoothing in private real estate funds, rather than of lower risk.

Risk measurement in real estate markets is important because if risk is understated, the portfolio allocation to private real estate will be too high and the rewards relative to risks will appear overstated.
