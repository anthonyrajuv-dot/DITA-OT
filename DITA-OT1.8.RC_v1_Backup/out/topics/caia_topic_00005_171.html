Exam Focus

This topic review provides the basics of the operational due diligence (ODD) that hedge fund investors should be aware of. Be sure to understand why ODD processes are different for private equity, hedge funds, and funds of hedge funds (FoFs). Understand how cash moves in and out of a hedge fund and how a hedge fund works with prime brokers and hedge fund administrators. Also important are knowledge of the compliance process, background checks, fund governance, and insurance considerations.
