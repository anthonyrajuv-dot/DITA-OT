Relative value multistrategy funds contain a combination of multiple arbitrage strategies. They are formed to increase fund capacity, increase diversification, and increase opportunities for investment due to the cyclical nature of markets.

The cross-sectional performance of relative value multistrategy funds indicated higher returns and lower volatility compared to global bonds. The return distribution of multistrategy funds had negative skewness and excess kurtosis. Also, correlations of multistrategy funds with global equities and high-yield bonds were high.
