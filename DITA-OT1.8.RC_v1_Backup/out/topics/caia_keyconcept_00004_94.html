LO 10.8

A service provider is a third-party firm (such as an auditor, bank, or fund administrator) providing investment and non-investment-related services to private equity firms and funds. In evaluating a service provider for the fund/firm, the investor is looking to evaluate the relationships of the provider with general partners, determine whether the provider has the appropriate size and capability to work with the fund, and assess the fees charged relative to the quality of the services provided.
