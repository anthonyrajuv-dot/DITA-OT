Exam Focus

The stages of the PE investment process include setting portfolio objectives, designing the portfolio, managing liquidity, selecting funds, monitoring, and actions/implementation. Be familiar with the challenges of balancing risk and return for PE investments and be able to explain how models such as the endowment model can be used to maximize diversification benefits and take advantage of illiquidity premiums. Understand the challenges (in terms of data needed and assumptions required) of applying modern portfolio theory to private equity. Be able to explain how the three primary forms of portfolio construction (top-down, bottom-up, and mixed) are different and how they can be used together.
