LO 16.4

Real estate style boxes offer another means of characterizing real estate investments. A real estate style box typically has nine boxes with core, value-added, and opportunistic on one axis and factors such as the type of market (i.e., primary, secondary, and tertiary) on the other. The box can be used to locate a particular property or property manager. Alternatively, expected returns, expected volatilities, expected alphas, and so on can be housed in each box.
