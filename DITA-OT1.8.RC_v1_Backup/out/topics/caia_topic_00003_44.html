Risk Management and Portfolio Management

Topics 7.1–7.4

Weight on Exam

5–10%

SchweserNotes™ Reference

Book 2, Pages 279–360

The risk management topic covers both qualitative and quantitative strategies for managing fund risk. First, understand the lessons learned from various hedge fund failures. Next, know the details of the investment process for an investor including an examination of investment process risk, operational risk, and a manager’s risk management process. One of the key lessons is that it is the investor’s responsibility to perform due diligence on a fund. The due diligence process is time consuming and expensive and therefore should result in higher alpha for investors. Know the seven phases to the due diligence process, paying particular attention to investment process and operational risks. Finally, understand how alpha and beta are used by a skilled portfolio manager.
