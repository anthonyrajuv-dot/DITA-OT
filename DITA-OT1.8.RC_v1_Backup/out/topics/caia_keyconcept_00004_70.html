LO 8.1

The main purpose of a benchmark is to serve as a point of reference for evaluating performance. A benchmark can also be used to determine incentive compensation, serve as marketing tool (in evaluating performance relative to a benchmark), and provide quantitative inputs into the strategic asset allocation decision process.

The steps to use a PE benchmark are (1) determine value and historical performance of PE asset, (2) select a suitable benchmark, and (3) calculate the performance differential between the asset and the benchmark.
