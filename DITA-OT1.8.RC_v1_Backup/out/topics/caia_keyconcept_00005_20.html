LO 16.5

A cap rate (capitalization rate) is the net operating income (NOI) of a real estate investment divided by the property value (i.e., appraised value or purchase price on an unlevered basis). It can reflect the return on the investment or can be used as a required rate of return to estimate the value of a real estate investment.

The investor can use the cap rate to solve for the value of the investment. The equation is:

property value = NOI / cap rate
