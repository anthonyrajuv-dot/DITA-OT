Exam Focus

Regulation and compliance are key issues in both traditional and alternative investments. In this topic review, we focus on the regulatory environment as it impacts the alternative investments industry. For the exam, understand recent changes in both the global and U.S. regulatory environments. Be very familiar with the requirements imposed by the Dodd-Frank Act for U.S. hedge funds. In addition, the legislated responsibilities of the chief compliance officer (CCO) and hedge fund managers should be well understood. Candidates should also be familiar with the European passport model offered in the Alternative Investment Fund Managers Directive and understand major differences in regulations for domestic and international hedge funds in the European Union (EU) and Asia.
