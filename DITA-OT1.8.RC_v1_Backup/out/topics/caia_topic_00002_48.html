LBO investments differ significantly from traditional investments in that they require activist, controlling investors and a substantial amount of leverage and are privately traded.

A management buyout (MBO) is an LBO led by the current managers of a company. A management buy-in (MBI) is an LBO led by an outside management team that replaces the companyâ€™s current managers.
