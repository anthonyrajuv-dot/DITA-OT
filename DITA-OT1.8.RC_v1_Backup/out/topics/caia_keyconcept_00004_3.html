Four factors influence the types of costs an institutional investor will incur:

  • Policy portfolio.
  • Size of the portfolio.
  • Investment vehicles used in the portfolio.
  • Investment model used.

Main investment models used by institutional investors include:

  • The traditional consultant model.
  • The internal chief investment officer (CIO) model.
  • The fund of funds model.
  • The outsourced CIO model.
