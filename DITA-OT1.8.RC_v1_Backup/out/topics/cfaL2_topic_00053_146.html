Exam Focus

After completing this reading, you should understand what algorithmic trading is and how it is defined. Be able to describe the different types of execution algorithms and high-frequency trading algorithms. You will want to be able to describe market fragmentation and its effects on how trades are placed. Also, be able to describe the use of technology in risk management and regulatory oversight. Finally, be sure that you are familiar with the issues and concerns related to the impact of algorithmic and high-frequency trading on securities markets.
