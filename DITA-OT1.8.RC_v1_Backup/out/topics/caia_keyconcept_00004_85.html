LO 9.7

The three broad categories of active involvement in monitoring include:

  • Active involvement in a fund’s governance process, such as negotiating changes in fund size, management fees, early commitment releases, involvement in decision making, and potentially terminating a fund management team.
  • Actions outside of a fund’s governance process such as LPs choosing not to commit to follow-on funding and potentially choosing to default on commitments.
  • Actions outside of fund investing such as co-investing and/or allocating funds to public, small-cap equities as proxies for PE investments.
