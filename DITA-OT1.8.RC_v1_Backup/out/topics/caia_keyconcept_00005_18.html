Styles are introduced into real estate portfolio analysis to help investors understand and control risks.

  • Assess performance. Styles help investors better understand the performance of the portfolio, including benchmarks and performance attribution.
  • Detect style drift. Recognizing the style and drifts from that style (called style drift) helps investors appreciate the riskiness of the portfolio over time.
