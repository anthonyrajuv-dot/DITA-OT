Exam Focus

This topic review discusses different approaches to risk measurement as well as mechanisms to manage/control risk. VaR is an important risk metric, and you should know different ways to compute it as well as pros and cons of different approaches. Also know the limitations of VaR as a risk metric and the variations of VaR. The discussion on scenario and sensitivity analysis is mostly qualitative. Finally, know the risk measures that are more relevant for different asset managers, such as banks, pension funds, et cetera.
