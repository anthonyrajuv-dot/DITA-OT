Exam Focus

The focus of this topic review is on products and strategies that earn returns based on volatilities, correlations, or dispersions of underlying assets. Focus on how to use options as a risk management tool and understand the impact of the Greeks (i.e., delta, gamma, vega, and theta) on investment returns for the exam. Understand the volatility products, especially VIX and correlation swaps, and be able to perform the relevant calculations. Finally, be able to clearly distinguish between vertical intra-asset, horizontal intra-asset, and inter-asset spreads.
