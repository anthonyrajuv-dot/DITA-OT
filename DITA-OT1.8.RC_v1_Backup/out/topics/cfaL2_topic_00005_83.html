Exam Focus

Information ratio is used to evaluate active managers and can be used to decide appropriate portfolio allocation for an investor. There are lots of formulae and linkages to be on top of for this reading. Understand the differences between the Sharpe ratio and the information ratio. Be able to describe the full fundamental law and what influences each of the components. Understand the application of the fundamental law in the context of market timing and sector rotation strategies. Finally, be aware of the limitations of the fundamental law.
