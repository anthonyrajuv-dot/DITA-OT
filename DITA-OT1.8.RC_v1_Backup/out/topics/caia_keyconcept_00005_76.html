LO 25.2

Managed futures investment strategies can be subdivided into four core dimensions: data sources, implementation style, strategy focus, and time horizon.

  • Strategies based on data sources can be technical or fundamental in nature.
  • Strategies based on implementation style can be classified as systematic or discretionary.
  • Strategies based on implementation strategy focus include momentum, mean reversion, global macro, relative value, carry trading, and multistrategy.
  • Strategies based on time horizon could range from high frequency to long term, with short term and medium term to complete the range.
