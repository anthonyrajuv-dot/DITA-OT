Benefits of private equity secondary investments on a stand-alone basis:

  • Higher visibility into the composition and performance of the underlying portfolio.
  • Lower blind pool risk.
  • Lesser J-curve effect.
  • Lower loss rates with less variability.
  • Access to funds or general partners that may not be accessible otherwise.

Benefits of secondary investments to a private equity portfolio:

  • Quicker portfolio build-up.
  • Improved diversification.
  • Smoother cash flow profile.

Compared to traditional private equity funds, private equity secondary funds have exhibited higher average IRRs, lower TVPI ratios, lower loss rates, faster return of cash, and less volatile returns with a narrower dispersion.
