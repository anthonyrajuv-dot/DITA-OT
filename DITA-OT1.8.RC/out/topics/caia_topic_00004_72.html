Exam Focus

This topic review covers the fundamental elements of endowments and foundations. Understand the characteristics of these two institutions and the differences that exist between them. Be able to describe the four foundation types. Ensure that you are able to calculate the change in endowment or foundation value and that you understand how spending rates, return targets, and intergenerational equity influence investment decisions. Understand and be able to discuss the six reasons large endowments have historically outperformed other investments as well as the risks of the endowment model.
