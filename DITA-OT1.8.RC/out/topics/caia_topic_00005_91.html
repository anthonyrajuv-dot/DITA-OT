Exam Focus

In this topic review, we examine how investors can access commodity beta through passive investments. Return to commodity beta is the passive risk-adjusted return earned from a long-only commodity position. In the past decade, the number of investment vehicles available to both institutions and individuals to obtain commodity exposure has greatly expanded. Know the various investment vehicles used to obtain commodity exposure. In addition, commodity indices are often used as a basis for many investment vehicles. Therefore, candidates should be well-versed on the three generations of available commodity indices and the return components that determine the overall performance of the indices.
