Exam Focus

Investing in traditional assets is facilitated by a deep body of theoretical and empirical research. However, investing in alternative assets requires a deviation from traditional analysis and management techniques. This topic review focuses on developing a framework for differentiating alternative assets from traditional assets. Understand the various categories of alternative assets and the underlying structures that make them unique. Know the distinctive return characteristics of alternative assets as well as the methods of analysis that differ from traditional investments. Finally, be able to explain the goals of alternative investing.
