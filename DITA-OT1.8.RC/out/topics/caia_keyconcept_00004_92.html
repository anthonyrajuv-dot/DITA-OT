LO 10.6

Although a desk review may be used, it is best practice to conduct an on-site meeting after the documents have all been collected and analyzed. An on-site meeting allows the investor to meet key personnel associated with the firm and fund and to review documents that the general partner may wish to physically keep in the office. The investor should have a detailed agenda going into the meeting that covers organizational reviews, operational practices, documentation content verification, and specific policies and procedures.
