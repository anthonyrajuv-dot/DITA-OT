This topic focuses on asset allocation. Know the steps in the allocation process. Understand the expected utility of an asset and mean-variance optimization. Using endowments and pension plans as examples, we examine how the unique characteristics of a portfolio help determine the optimal asset allocation. Understand how the endowment model has benefited portfolios but is risky during periods of financial stress due to illiquidity. Also, understand the primary differences between defined benefit and defined contribution pension plans and how differing asset allocation strategies perform in various market conditions. Understand the basics of annuities. Also, know that sovereign wealth funds (SWFs) now have trillions of dollars under management. Know the various types of SWFs and issues specific to these government funds. Also, understand the specific investment issues and constraints that affect family offices.
