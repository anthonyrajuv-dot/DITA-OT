Exam Focus

Covered in this topic review are the fundamental elements of tactical asset allocation, mean-variance extensions, risk budgeting, risk parity, and factor investing. Be able to apply these topics with regard to traditional assets and alternative investments. Ensure that you are able to adjust the mean-variance approach to account for illiquidity, issues related to factor exposures, and estimation risk. Understand and be able to discuss the different methods used to create an optimal portfolio based on various risk exposures.
