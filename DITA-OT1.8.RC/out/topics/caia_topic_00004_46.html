Exam Focus

This topic review discusses the research conclusions from a 2015 study by Cambridge Associates on private equity co-investments. In this study, the researchers analyzed the returns of more than 40 funds and funds of funds managing over 500 co-investments. For the exam, understand the different types of co-investment structures and their risk-return profiles. Be able to describe the benefits and challenges of co-investments, as well as key factors that improve outcomes from co-investments.
