LO 21.5

IP is growing in significance and possesses most of the following six common characteristics of real assets.

  • Low operating risk.
  • Positive correlation with U.S. or European inflation.
  • Value preservation during macroeconomic instability.
  • Benefit from input scarcity in energy, manufacturing, and agricultural sectors.
  • Essential part of economic infrastructure, IP is a significant part of GDP.

Risk and return properties suitable for funding long-term liabilities.
