Put-call parity for European options says that the cost of a fiduciary call must be equal to the cost of a protective put:

Put-call parity can be used to demonstrate how to create synthetic instruments. For example, a synthetic call is a long put, a long stock position, and a short position in a pure-discount risk-free bond. Investors create these synthetic securities to exploit relative mispricing among the four securities.
