LO 13.6

The three formal approaches to modeling private equity cash flow projections include:

  • Estimates (used for a 3–6 month time horizon).
  • Forecasts (used for a 1–2 year time horizon).
  • Scenarios (used for 2 years and beyond).

Monte Carlo simulation is a valuable tool used to provide estimates. Forecasts represent the most likely outcome of the future, while scenarios represent a set of possible situations that help entities plan for an inevitably uncertain future.
