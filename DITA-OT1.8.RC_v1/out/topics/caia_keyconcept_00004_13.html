LO DCCS.5

Asset owners in the fixed income, equities, private equity, property, and infrastructure areas should:

  • Engage with policy makers in the medium to long term.
  • Engage with companies in the medium term.
  • Integrate climate change investment in portfolio decisions in the medium to long term.
  • Integrate low-carbon solutions in the short term.

Asset owners of fixed income and equities are encouraged to avoid high-carbon companies in the short term. conditions.

Convex strategies (CPPI, OBPI) are trend-following and outperform both buy-and-hold and concave strategies in trending (bull or bear) markets.

Concave strategies (constant mix) are contrarian, and outperform both buy-and-hold and convex strategies in flat, but oscillating markets.
