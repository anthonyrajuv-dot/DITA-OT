Exam Focus

This topic review introduces real estate as a potential investment for institutional investors. For the exam, know the different types of unlisted and listed real estate investments and be able to identify their distinct characteristics. Understand what features makes each investment type attractive to investors, including tradability, tax benefits, fees, and leverage. Be prepared to discuss market-based versus appraisal-based returns and the observed volatilities and pricing of investments. Finally, be able to identify the characteristics of arbitrage, liquidity, and segmentation as they relate to exchange-traded funds and real estate investment trusts.
