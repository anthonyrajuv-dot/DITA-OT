LO 23.3

The primary structure of a venture capital fund is a limited partnership with the venture capitalist as the general partner and the other investors as limited partners. The structure provides limited liability to investors. Limited liability means that the limited partners can only lose up to the value of their investment; there is no personal liability for the activities of the fund.
