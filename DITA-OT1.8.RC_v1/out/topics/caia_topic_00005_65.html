Exam Focus

Intellectual property (IP) is an intangible asset with ownership rights that can be transferred. Film production and distribution, art, and patents are discussed in this topic review. Understand the concept of unbundling IP from a firm or institution. Know the life cycle of film production and the risks and rewards of investing in films. Also, understand the various equity and debt financing structures used to fund films. Understand the metrics used to relate returns to investments in films. Understand both financial and nonfinancial reasons investors include art in investment portfolios. Understand the unique issues and risks associated with investments in patents, how investors access patent investments, and how they protect those investments once licensed or acquired. Finally, understand the arguments for and against classifying intellectual property as a real asset.
