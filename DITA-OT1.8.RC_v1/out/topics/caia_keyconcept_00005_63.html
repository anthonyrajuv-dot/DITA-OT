LO 23.3

Directional commodity strategies establish long or short positions in commodity derivatives based on forecasts of commodity price changes. Less common strategies use physical commodities, ETFs, and commodity-based equities.

Fundamental directional strategies use supply and demand analysis as well as an analysis of fundamental economic factors to determine the direction of commodity prices.

Quantitative directional strategies identify undervalued or overvalued commodities using technical and quantitative indicators.
