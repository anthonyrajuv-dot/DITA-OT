LO 3.3

The endowment model considers the need to meet high return targets in order for the endowment to exist in perpetuity. To meet high return targets, alternative investments often constitute a large percentage of the asset allocation for endowments, while corporate and foreign fixed-income investments are largely not used. Empirical evidence indicates large endowments earned substantially higher returns than domestic equity and fixed-income investment returns. Positive correlations exist between the size of the endowment, the returns generated, and the allocation to alternative investments.
