Exam Focus

This topic review covers the asset allocation process and the mean-variance model. Understand the importance of asset allocation and the five steps of the asset allocation process. Be able to describe the categories of asset owners and discuss the objectives and constraints of asset owners. Understand investment policy objectives and be able to evaluate investor utility based on expected return and variance. Describe internal and external investment policy constraints as well as the steps taken to prepare an investment policy statement and develop a strategic asset allocation. Finally, be able to describe and discuss the implementation of an investment policy statement.
