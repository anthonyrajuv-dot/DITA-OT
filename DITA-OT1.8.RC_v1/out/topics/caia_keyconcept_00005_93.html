LO 31.5

A review of civil, criminal, and regulatory actions is an important part of due diligence because (1) it provides an insight into the manager’s character, and (2) legal and regulatory issues are time consuming.

Employee turnover leads to the following issues: (1) fund manager is forced to spend time and resources finding and training new employees, (2) former employees may have been responsible for the fund’s competitive advantage, and (3) high levels of turnover may reflect a volatile work environment.

The investor relations team is the investor’s link for handling issues related to setting up meetings, handling withdrawal requests, processing checks, and conveying performance information.

Business continuity planning gives the fund manager a course of action for continuing to manage the fund in the event that a disaster shuts down a fund’s trading and operations.
