LO 17.2

Global macro funds are opportunistic in nature, use a top-down approach, and can invest anywhere in the world based on forecasts of economic growth, currency movements, and changes in interest rates. Global macro strategies are most profitable when market events (i.e., exchange rates, interest rates, inflation rates, or other macroeconomic factors) cause increased uncertainty and/or volatility. The three primary risks of global macro funds include market risk, event risk, and leverage risk.
