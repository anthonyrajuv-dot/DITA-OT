Economic Value Determinants of REITs

National GDP growth is the largest driver of economic value for all REIT types. Overall growth in the economy means more jobs, more need for office space, more disposable income, more growth in shopping centers, more demand for hotel rooms from business and leisure travellers, and so on.

In addition to national GDP growth, there are four major economic factors that impact REITs, as shown in Figure 1.
