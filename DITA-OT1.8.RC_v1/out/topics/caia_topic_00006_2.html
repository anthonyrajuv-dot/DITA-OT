Ethics is 10% of the constructed response (i.e., essay) portion of the Level II examination and is extremely important to your overall success. Ethics can be tricky, and small details can be important on ethics questions. Be prepared. In addition to starting early, study the ethics material more than once. Ethics is one of the keys to passing the exam.

We recommend you read the Standards of Practice Handbook, which is available for free in electronic form on the CAIA Association website. Although we are very proud of our reviews of the ethics material, constructed response questions require a deep knowledge of the material. You will be much better off if you read both our summaries of the Standards and the original handbook and all the examples presented in it.
