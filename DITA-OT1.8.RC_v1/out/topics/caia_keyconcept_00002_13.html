LO 17.6

  • Trend-following strategies refer to trading strategies that attempt to exploit predictable price patterns after the trend has begun.
  • Non-trend-following strategies attempt to profit from inconsistencies in market prices.
  • Countertrend strategies attempt to profit by trading against the current trend.
  • Relative value strategies attempt to exploit price inefficiencies between two related futures contracts.
  • Buy (sell) when the price is above (below) a simple, weighted, or exponential moving average.
  • Common channel breakout strategies indicate a buy (sell) signal is generated when the price is above (below) the upper (lower) bound.
