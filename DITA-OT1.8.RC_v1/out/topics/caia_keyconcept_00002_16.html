LO 18.1

Event-driven strategies have similar payoffs to insurance and binary options.

  • The economic process of earning relatively small risk premiums for protection against large losses of unfavorable events is known as selling insurance.
  • Under the binary call option view, the event-driven fund gains if the event takes place and the option is in-the-money and loses if the event fails to occur and the option expires worthless.
  • Under the binary put option view, event-driven funds write put options and have payouts consistent with writing out-of-the-money options.
