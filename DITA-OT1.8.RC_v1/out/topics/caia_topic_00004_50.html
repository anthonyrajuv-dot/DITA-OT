Warm-Up: Aging Populations

In many countries, longer life expectancy presents significant challenges from both a social and regulatory perspective. In addition, an increase in the average age of retirees creates longevity risk for pension plans and annuities. This risk jeopardizes employee retirement savings because pensions and annuities may be unable to make benefit payments over a longer time horizon than expected.

Overall, longevity risk is a significant concern for the broader financial system. A one-year increase to life expectancy could increase the present value of defined benefit (DB) plan liabilities by 3% to 4%.
