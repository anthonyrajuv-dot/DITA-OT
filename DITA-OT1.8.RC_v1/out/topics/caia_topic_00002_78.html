LO 28.6

Eight motivations for investing in structured products:

  • Enhanced returns.
  • Enhanced liquidity.
  • Improved risk management.
  • Improved diversification.
  • Improved access to top managers.
  • Regulatory advantages.
  • Reduced income taxes.
  • Reduced transaction taxes.

Motivations for issuing structured products:

  • Fee revenue and profitability.
  • Beneficial source of financing (i.e., lower financing costs, superior risk exposures, desirable maturities).
