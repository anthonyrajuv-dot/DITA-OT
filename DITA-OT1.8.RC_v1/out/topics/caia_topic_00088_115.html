Exam Focus

Convertible arbitrage and equity market-neutral investment strategies both focus on relative value and market mispricings of related securities. The basics of arbitrage are discussed in this topic review, and you should have a strong understanding of the factors that limit arbitrage as well as the concept of efficiently inefficient markets. A detailed account of convertible arbitrage is provided, and you should be comfortable with all of the terminology and calculations pertaining to convertible bonds as well as how to value convertible bonds using both the component approach and the binomial model. Finally, you should have a reasonable knowledge of the various equity market-neutral investment strategies as well as the implementation process. Understanding the sources of risk and return with pairs trading is also important.
