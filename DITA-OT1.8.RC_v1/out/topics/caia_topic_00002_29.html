LO 20.4

  • Multiple-factor scoring models generate a single trading signal based on combining scores from numerous anomalies.
  • Pairs trading strategies match stocks based on systematic risk. A long position is established in the undervalued stock, and a short position is held in the overpriced stock. The hedge fund profits if the stock prices converge.
  • Portfolios that allow short sales have higher expected returns and alphas for every level of risk tracking than long only portfolios.
  • The limits to arbitrage are related to time and size constraints of hedge fund managers, market structures, and inabilities to establish short positions due to market restrictions or lack of supply of shares.
