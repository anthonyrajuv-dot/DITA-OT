Unit 1: Broker and Agent Responsibilities and Common Legal Issues

  1. Legal Issues for Licensees
    1. Mistakes happen

      Learning how to avoid them is critical and can reduce the risk of getting sued.

      1. The slightest mistake could end up costing you a lot of money
      2. It could cause a lot of stress
      3. It could tarnish your reputation
    2. Lean on the side of preparedness
      1. Accidents can still happen.
      2. Learning how to avoid the most common mistakes can significantly reduce the risk of being sued
      3. Educate yourself to lean on the side of preparedness.
    3. Top for

      The following are the top reasons agents find themselves in trouble. Source: Realtor.com/Inman News

      1. Failing to disclose a property defect
        1. Clients who discover defects after signing the papers will be quick to blame the real estate agent. Every bit of damage and every defect found on the property should be thoroughly documented.
        2. Defects can include construction issues, improvements without permits, leaks, cracking, noise or nuisances.
        3. When client sue a real estate agent for failing to disclose a property defect, they have to prove the agent knew or should have known about the defect and failed to disclose it.
        4. Be sure to perform a thorough inspection of the property, and have your clients sign a statement that documents their awareness of any issues (disclosure statement)

        CLASS DISCUSSION: What is your responsibility for disclosure? How will you sure those responsibilities are met?

      2. Breach of duty
        1. One of the most common lawsuits brought against real estate agents is for breach of fiduciary duty.
        2. Real estate agents know they must always act in the best interest of the client.
        3. Always remember who you represent.
        4. If faced with a question of what you should do in any given situation, start with who you represent and then apply your fiduciary duties to the question at hand.
