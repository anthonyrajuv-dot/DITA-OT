Private equity is an important part of the Level I exam. In this section, we cover the fundamental structure of various private equity investments including venture capital, leveraged buyouts, distressed debt, and mezzanine debt, as well as trends in private equity investments. Much of the material is straightforward, so make sure you get these points on the exam.
