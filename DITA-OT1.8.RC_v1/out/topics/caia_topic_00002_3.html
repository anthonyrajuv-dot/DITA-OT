LO 16.3

The five major CAIA classifications of hedge fund strategies are:

  • Macro and Managed Futures.
  • Event-Driven.
  • Relative Value.
  • Equity.
  • Funds of Funds.

Single-manager funds have underlying investments that do not consist of other hedge funds. Multistrategy funds are single-manager hedge funds with investments that include a variety of strategies. Funds of funds (FOFs) invest in a diversified group of single-manager funds and charge a second layer of fees to compensate the manager for portfolio construction, monitoring, and oversight.
