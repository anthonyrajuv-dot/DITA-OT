LO 9.6

Transparency is a critical element of private equity fund reporting, as the need for timely and accurate information is critical for investors looking to commit large amounts of capital. While a GP will want to share critical information with investors in order to secure funding, this must be balanced with concerns over providing too much information such that competitors can take advantage. From the LP side, while more information is generally preferable, access to investment opportunities may be compromised by deep-pocketed LPs who have more information due to enhanced disclosures.
