The NPI and the FTSE NAREIT U.S. Real Estate Index Series are used as proxies for smoothed and unsmoothed data, respectively. The mean returns are somewhat similar between the two indices, but the standard deviation of the smoothed NPI is substantially lower. Also, the NPI is unleveraged, while the NAREIT Index reflects leverage.

The volatility of the smoothed NPI increases substantially when the data is unsmoothed.
