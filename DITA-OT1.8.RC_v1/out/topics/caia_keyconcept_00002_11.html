LO 17.4

  • Managed futures strategies are typically based on technical analysis and allow investors higher return investment opportunities of actively managed future contracts.
  • The Commodity Exchange Act (CEA) set standards for financial reporting, disclosure, and recordkeeping.
  • Commodity Pool Operators (CPOs) are general partners in a commodity pool, who typically hire professional money managers called Commodity Trading Advisors (CTAs) to manage the funds.
  • The managed futures industry comprises three segments: (1) public commodity pools, (2) private commodity pools, and (3) individually managed accounts.
