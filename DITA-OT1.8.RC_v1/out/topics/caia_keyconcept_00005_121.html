LO 32.3

Key observations from the historical performance for FoFs from 1990–2014 include the following:

  • Hedge fund returns were much lower over the 2000–2014 period as compared to the 1990–1999 period.
  • Hedge fund standard deviations were higher over the 2000–2014 period as compared to the 1990–1999 period.
  • Hedge fund Sharpe ratios were much lower over the 2000–2014 period as compared to the 1990–1999 period.
  • The declining performance over time suggests investors should lower expectations of future hedge fund performance.
