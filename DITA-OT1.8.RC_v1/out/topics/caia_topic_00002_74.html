LO 28.2

In a tax-free investment, the after-tax return on wrappers equals the investment return.

In a fully taxed investment, the after-tax return on wrappers equals the investment return less applicable taxes.

In a tax-deferral investment, investors delay paying taxes on investment gains when they are accrued until the gains are paid to the investor.

In a tax-deferred and tax-deductible investment, investors are able to both delay paying taxes on investment gains and deduct their investment contributions for tax purposes.
