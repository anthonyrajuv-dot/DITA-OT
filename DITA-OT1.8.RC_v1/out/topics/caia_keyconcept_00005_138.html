Fund governance refers to the interconnected system of procedures and controls that determine fund transparency, independence, and oversight.

Five common operations-related committees for hedge funds are (1) operations committee, (2) valuation committee, (3) disaster recovery and business continuity committee, (4) best execution committee, and (5) compliance committee.
