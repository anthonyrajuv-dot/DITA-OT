LOS 45.d: Explain how interest rate volatility affects the value of a callable or putable bond.

CFAÂ® Program Curriculum, Volume 5, page 324

Option values are positively related to the volatility of their underlying. Accordingly, when interest rate volatility increases, the values of both call and put options increase. The value of a straight bond is affected by changes in the level of interest rate but is unaffected by changes in the volatility of interest rate.

When interest rate volatility increases, the value of a callable bond (where the investor is short the call option) decreases and the value of a putable bond (where the investor is long the put option) increases.
