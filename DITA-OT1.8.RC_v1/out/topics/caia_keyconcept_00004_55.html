LO 6.2

Family offices have diverse goals that could include intergenerational wealth transfer, philanthropy, and maintaining the lifestyle needs of family members.

Benefits of family offices include conflict-free advice, centralization of portfolio management, and understanding of the unique needs of different family members.

In the United States, family offices that limit membership to lineal descendants of the founder and their spouses do not fall under SEC regulation. Family offices that accept funds from non-family members are regulated, which can substantially increase their management costs.
