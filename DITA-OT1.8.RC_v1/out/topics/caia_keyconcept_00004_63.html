The organized PE market offers professional management provided by intermediaries. The informal PE market represents angel capital with funding often coming from the founderâ€™s own resources. Most investors enter into private equity through the organized PE market.

The three primary types of PE investments are venture capital (for funding young, high growth potential companies), buyouts (debt and equity used to buy shares of an established business), and mezzanine (used to finance transitions or expansions of established, privately held companies). Rescue and replacement capital are other PE investment strategies.
