Exam Focus

This topic review provides a detailed account of managed futures and the material is primarily nontechnical in nature and focusing on issues such as the structure, foundations, and strategies. Pay particular attention to the four dimensions of managed futures investment strategies given the amount of detail that is covered. You should also understand the benefits of commodity trading advisors (CTAs) because the subsequent topic is completely devoted to CTAs. Finally, be able to perform the calculations related to market divergence (signal-to-noise ratio and market divergence index) and position sizing (two approaches).
