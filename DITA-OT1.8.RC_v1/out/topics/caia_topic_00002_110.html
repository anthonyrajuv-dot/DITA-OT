Exam Focus

This topic review presents several examples of recent hedge fund collapses. You need to have an understanding of the specific hedge fund strategies employed by these funds and the factors that contributed to their demise. Know how managers use leverage to increase return on assets, and the risks associated with high levels of leverage. Understand how behavioral biases contributed to the collapses of some of these funds. You should be familiar with the impact that new trading technologies have had on financial crises, and be knowledgeable of recent fund failures that were due to fraud. Finally, be able to identify the broad conclusions that can be drawn from hedge fund failures.
