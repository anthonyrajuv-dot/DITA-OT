LO 21.4

  • Due to a median minimum investment size of $500,000, creating a diversified portfolio of hedge funds would require investing at least $10 million.
  • The second layer of fees charged by an FOF should be compared to the cost to an investor of building a diversified portfolio of hedge funds. The cost of creating and maintaining an internal evaluation system has been estimated to be $1 million. This suggests a hedge fund portfolio would need to be at least $50 million to make independent management cost-effective compared to FOF investing.
