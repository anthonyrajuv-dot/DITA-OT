LO 3.4

Six reasons why large endowments using the endowment model have outperformed other investors are:

  • Aggressive tactical asset allocation.
  • Superior investment manager research.
  • First-mover advantage.
  • Access to a network of talented alumni.
  • Acceptance of liquidity risk.
  • Sophisticated investment staff and board oversight.

Investment return attribution consists of contributions from strategic asset allocation, security selection, and tactical allocation. The increased reliance of endowments on security selection and tactical asset allocation makes it difficult for other investors and funds to accurately replicate their investment strategy.
