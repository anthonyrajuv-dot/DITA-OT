LO 11.1

The primary steps in the investment process are:

Setting portfolio objectives.

Portfolio design.

Managing liquidity.

Fund selection.

Monitoring.

Active management and implementation.

As with any investment asset class, the goal with private equity is to find a balance between risk and return. Standard models are difficult to apply to private equity because of challenges in determining a risk premium and correlations with other asset classes. The endowment model looks to maximize risk-adjusted investment return through diversification across asset classes that have low/negative correlations to each other, while also shifting funds towards more illiquid assets that offer the potential for higher returns.
